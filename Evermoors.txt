====== Evermoors ======
Created Friday 13 March 2020

__Vast, unsettled area of fog-shrouded hills, cold bogs, rocky ridges, and small peaks.__

**survival:** ettins, trolls, and witches dwell here in great numbers.

If the party ever gets close to the Evermoors, the most perceptive member spots a castle that seems to float on the clouds about a mile over the deepest parts of the bog land.

It is [[:Lyn Armaal]] , [[:Countess Sansuri]] 's float cloud castle.
