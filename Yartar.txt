====== Yartar ======
Created Friday 13 March 2020

__The fortified city of Yartar stands on the east bank of the Desarin River. On the west bank is a walled citadel, and between them spanning the river is a large stone bridge.__

Inside: __Yartar is a bustling mercantile metropolis. Store and shops line every block and smelly, brightly-dressed individuals of myriad races crowd the main streets. You get the sense you could find anything here, if only you knew where to look.__

Once on the eastern side of the gate: __While the western road is laid stone, the road eastward out of the city is naught but dirt and gravel.__

Travelers must pass through inspections to cross the bridge.
  Have the guards at the checkpoint act shadily.
  Give a check or the players have money stolen from them.

Yartar is home to an all-female thieve's guild the [[:Hand of Yartar]]

Yartar is also the headquarters of the [[:Lionshield Coster]]
  Any players who visit the headquarters and seem well off are offered a loan of supplies and 5,000 gold pieces to open a franchise. 

The esteemed [[:Lord Drylund]] owns and runs a casino on his river boat, the [[:]][[:Golden Goose|Golden Goose]]
