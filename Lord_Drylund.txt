====== Lord Drylund ======
Created Friday 26 February 2021

Human Male
Lord Drylund is a man of gaudy taste. He wears brightly-colored robes and jewelry in conflicting prints, patterns, and designs.

Lord Kasper Drylund dreams of the day when he rules Yartar as its Waterbaron.
He also is a member of the [[:Kraken Society]] and receives occasional telepathic static from the Kraken [[:Slarkrethel]]
