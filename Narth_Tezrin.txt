====== Narth Tezrin ======
Created Saturday 10 August 2019

Narth is a clerk and co-owner at the [[:Lionshield Coster]] in [[:Triboar]]
He has an adventerous spirit and wants to impress [[:Alaestra Ulgar]] who he has a crush on.

**ideal** The bigger the risk, the greater the reward.
**bond** I adore my colleague Alaestra, and I'd like to do something to impress her.
**flaw** I'll risk life and limb to become a legend.
