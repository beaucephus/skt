====== Tibbers ======
Created Sunday 09 August 2020

Tibbers is a tabby tressym who was being cared for by [[:Krowen Valharrow]] the arch-mage in charge at the Moongleam tower in [[:Everlund]]
Tibbers was picked up by [[:Reed Osborn]] who cares for him now.
