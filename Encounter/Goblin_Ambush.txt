====== Goblin Ambush ======
Created Saturday 25 May 2019

''Everybody hears bird calls.''
''DC10 survival/nature check to recognize the calls are out-of-season.''

4 goblins ambush the party on the way to [[::Nightstone|Nightstone]]
2 use shortbows from the forest, full cover except when they shoot,
2 rush into melee with scimitars.

HP 7

+2 damage

scimitar
