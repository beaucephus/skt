====== Citadel Felbarr ======
Created Monday 02 September 2019

__This formidable dwarven fortress-city lies beneath the__ [[:Rauvin Mountains]] __. An elevated road sided by 100-foot hight cliffs winds and passes through the mountains into the 2 enourmous gates. (__named the "Hammer" and the "Anvil")
2 battlements stand atop each side of the gates. Guards stand at the ready to rain arrows, pitch, and fire on would-be invaders.

Run [[:Encounter:Felbarr Summons]] the first time tha players enter the city.
