====== Anemone Chest ======
Created Saturday 06 February 2021

The storm giants cultivate 20 foot anemones that can survive outside water and have small cavities in their centers for protected storage.
A storm giant knows the right location to touch the anemone to open it.
Touching the anemone requires DC10 DEX save or take 2d10 poison dmg. 
DC15 nature to open the chest successfully. On a failed check, make the DEX save.

AC 5
HP 20
Attacks require the DEX save
