====== Count Thullen ======
Created Saturday 12 September 2020

Count Thullen is a cloud giant male

He is the opposite to his sister [[:Countess Sansuri]] in many ways.

He is gentle and wise, not easily moved to violence. Only entering battle at the endangerment of his sister's children.
Thullen does not wear a mask.
page 198 for stats

**Developments**
Thullen tries to reason with the party as long as they don't harm Sansuri's children.
He knows the location of Sansuri's Conch of Teleportation(the tallest tower bedchamber, 33), but doesn't know exactly where
