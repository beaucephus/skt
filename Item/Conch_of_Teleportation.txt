====== Conch of Teleportation ======
Created Sunday 29 March 2020

This ordinary, albeit rather large, conch shell has been inscribed with a rune. The conch measures 2.5 feet long and weighs 20 pounds.

**history:** The run is an uvar rune. It signifies storms. 

As an action, you can cast teleport by blowing into the shell. The desitination is always [[:Maelstrom]]
This effect recharges at dawn.
