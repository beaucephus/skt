====== Shalamayne ======
Created Monday 03 June 2019

__This thick and heavy dwarven blade has a small hole worked into he middle of the blade near the crossguard.__
If an attuned creature grasps the handle, a small red flame erupts in the hole, emitting dim light in a 10-foot-radius. Creatures in this light gain advantage on saving throws to avoid being frightened. 

requires attunement
