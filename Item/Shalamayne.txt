====== Shalamayne ======
Created Monday 03 June 2019

__This thick, heavy dwarven blade has a small cavity worked into he middle of the blade near the quillon.__
If an attuned creature grasps the grip, a flame ignites in the cavity, emitting dim light in a 10 foot radius. While within this light, Shalamayne's weilder and their allies are immune to the frightened condition. 

requires attunement
