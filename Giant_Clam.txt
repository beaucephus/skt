====== Giant Clam ======
Created Saturday 06 February 2021

As an action a character can pry open a giant clam.
DC16 athletics

roll 1d100 for the contents of the clam
01-25 Clam meat
26-75 A pink grapefruit-sized pearl (500gp)
76-90 A black grapefruit-sized pearl (5,000gp)
91-98 A violet grapefruit-sized [[:Item:Pearl of Power]]
99-100 A clear grapefruit-sized [[:Item:Crystal Ball]]
