====== Olostin's Hold ======
Created Friday 13 March 2020

__Small fortified keep standing on the northern side of the Evermoor Way between Yartar and Everlund.__

Village includes a small market, smithy, caravan supplier and an inn called the Headless Troll.

If the party decides to stay the night
  Pick one of the traverlers options in [[:Wilderness Encounter:Travelers]]
